NtReplyPort(



  IN HANDLE               PortHandle,
  IN PLPC_MESSAGE         Reply );



NtReplyPort can be used by both sides of LPC connection.
  • PortHandle     HANDLE to Port Object.
  • Reply     Pointer to LPC_MESSAGE structure.
